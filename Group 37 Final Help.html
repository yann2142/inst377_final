How to Use ShelfMatch

Welcome to ShelfMatch! Here's how you can use the platform:

  1. Enter your search criteria such as book title, author, or genre in the form provided.
  2. You can also exclude certain keywords to avoid sensitive content in your search results.
  3. Click the search button to get personalized book recommendations without spoilers.
