About ShelfMatch

ShelfMatch is a book recommendation platform designed to help readers find books based on their personal preferences while excluding sensitive content. We provide an intuitive interface where users can search for books by title, author, or genre, and further filter results by excluding certain topics. Our goal is to offer an enjoyable and safe book browsing experience.
